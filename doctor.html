About the Doctor

A Specialist You Can Trust

Dr. Geetika Garg is a highly experienced Internal Medicine Specialist and General Physician at Prime Healthcare, Sector 27, Chandigarh. An MD Medicine Gold Medalist, she completed her MBBS from Government Medical College & Hospital, Patiala (2007) and her MD from Subharti Institute of Medical Sciences, Meerut (2013).

With 19 years of overall experience — including 11 years as a specialist — she has previously served at premier institutions including AIIMS, PGIMER, Govt. Medical College Sector 32, and Civil Hospital, Mohali. She is known for her patient-centred approach, taking the time to listen carefully and explain diagnoses clearly.
